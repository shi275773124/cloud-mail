Common questions before you ask for a quote.

What is the MOQ?

It depends on the item. Public supplier signals show some coffee bags and stickers can start around 500 pcs, but we confirm each quote before you order.

Can I start with one item?

Yes. You can request only coffee bags, only sleeves, only stickers, or a full opening kit.

Can you help with logo placement?

Yes. If your logo is not print-ready, mark “need design help” in the form.

Are the materials food-contact safe?

We confirm material and supplier statements per item. Do not assume until the quote is verified.
